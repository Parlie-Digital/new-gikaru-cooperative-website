Welcome to New Gikaru Farmers Cooperative Society Ltd.

Enhancing production of quality coffee with effective and efficient Methods.

Our Story

New Gikaru Farmersâ€™ Cooperative Society Ltd. is a five wet mill cooperative society, located in Mukiurwe-ini Sub County in Nyeri County, Kenya.Geographically it lies between the Eastern Aberdare Mountain range and Western slopes of Mt. Kenya.

  • Year of Establishment: 2001 .
  • Location: Mukiurwe-ini Sub County, Nyeri County, Kenya .
  • Certification: Fairtrade Certified since 2011

We aggregate coffee from small family farm,Process it for marketing.Our objective is to promote the economic interest and general welfare of our members in accordance with the cooperative principles.

Our Vision and Mission

  • Vision: To be the best managed and leading coffee and marketing cooperative society in Kenya.
  • Mission: To provide effective and efficient services to members and enhance production of quality coffee.

Core values

Our Core Values

Quality

We are committed to producing the highest quality coffee, ensuring every bean meets our stringent standards.

Innovations

We continuously seek innovative methods and technologies to improve our coffee production and operations.

Transparency

We maintain open and clear communication with our farmers and customers, building trust and reliability.

Fairness

We ensure fair trade practices, providing equitable opportunities and fair compensation for our farmers.

Community

We support and empower our local community, fostering growth and development through cooperative efforts.

Sustainability

We encourage our farmers to practice sustainable farming methods to protect their environment and ensure the future of coffee production.
