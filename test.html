Enhancing production of quality coffee with effective and efficient services.

About Us

New Gikaru Farmers Cooperative Society Ltd was established in 2001 in Mukiurwe-ini Sub County, Nyeri County, Kenya. We are Fairtrade Certified since 2011. Our vision is to be the best managed and leading coffee marketing cooperative society in Kenya.

Our core values include honesty, transparency, openness, accountability, caring for one another, and social responsibility.

Our Coffee

We produce 100% Arabica Speciality coffee grown between the eastern Aberdare mountain range and the western slopes of Mt. Kenya. Our coffee is grown in rich volcanic soils at an altitude of 1400-1600 meters, fully washed, and sun-dried on raised tables.

Key Initiatives

  • Gender Equality: We eliminate gender disparities and give women equal opportunities. Four out of six managerial positions are held by women.
  • Quality, Productivity & Sustainability: We implement quality production and sustainability projects, maintain certified coffee nursery, and uphold Fairtrade certification standards.
  • Diversification/Support for Women and Youth: We offer training and support, including initial capital for youth.
  • Use of Solar Energy: We use renewable energy systems, including a coffee solar drier.
